Our services include;

The purpose of engine oil is to keep the internal parts of your car’s engine lubricated and cool. It keeps the moving parts from grinding against each other causing wear and damage. Without frequent oil changes, dirt and sludge can build up in the engine, and old, dirty oil won’t lubricate the moving parts as well as new, fresh oil will. Dirty oil leads to serious damage, and if things get bad enough, there may be an engine replacement in your future.

Maintenance schedules offered by car manufacturers always include the transmission. As stated earlier, transmission fluid plays a critical role in how a transmission functions and the car part longevity. Like engine oil, transmission fluid should be checked and changed on a regular basis; however, the interval is different for all vehicles and dependent on the transmission and fluid type as well as use.

Your exhaust system plays a vital role in the overall performance of your vehicle. It’s composed of tubing intended to expel exhaust gases away from the controlled combustion taking place inside your engine. By speeding up the removal of these waste gases, you streamline your motor’s performance for increases in horsepower, gas mileage and overall efficiency. And, whether you’re looking for parts like an enhanced muffler, catalytic converter or new headers, we are here and ready for your vehicles repairs and/or upgrades.

Routine inspections keep your vehicle stopping safer, longer. How often you have to change your brakes depends on a lot of things: driving conditions, rotor thickness, quality of brake pads, and more. A routine brake inspection with every other oil change makes certain that your braking system is safe. If you wait too long between services, you might have to get your brake rotors machined or even replaced, which gets expensive. So stop in and let us take care of your routine brake check-ups before you can’t stop anymore.

Timing belt maintenance is extremely important! These models utilize an interference-type, non-free-wheeling engine. If the timing belt breaks, the valves in the cylinder head may strike the pistons, causing potentially serious (also time-consuming and expensive) engine damage.
Tune-ups can prevent a myriad of problems from occurring on a vehicle. Tune-ups are needed periodically as according to the manufacturer’s recommendations to ensure an automobile runs as expected.

A fuel injector is a valve that delivers fuel to a vehicle’s engine. All modern vehicles come with fuel injection systems and can become dirty and clogged over time, which will lead to poor performance, bad gas mileage, and even dirty exhaust emissions. If a fuel injector is dirty or clogged, your engine won’t get the necessary flow of fuel to mix with the air, which will lead to poor acceleration, engine performance, and gas mileage.Fuel injection service can be done with leaving the fuel injector in the vehicle or taking it out. Almost all of the time, fuel injection service can be done with the fuel injector still in the car, unless it needs to be replaced. Come by and let us check your Fuel Injection System and give you a piece of mind.
